# Skill: knowledge-feedback-skill v1

## Applies To

- KnowledgeFeedbackAgent

## Methodology

- Convert accepted cases into positive testing examples.
- Convert rejected cases and review comments into negative examples or
  TestStrategyNote candidates.
- Convert failures and reports into draft TestKnowledgeCard candidates such as
  BugPattern, CoverageGap, or regression reminders.
- Preserve source evidence and human review status before reuse.

## Input Contract

Accept accepted or rejected GeneratedCaseCandidate records, TestCase summaries,
ReviewHistory comments, FailureAnalysis summaries, Report summaries, and
normalized KnowledgeEvidence. Inputs must be safe to show and prompt-eligible
when copied into model prompts.

## Output Contract

Return draft knowledge feedback entries with feedback type, draft knowledge
type, source entity type, source entity id, source span or hash, recommendation,
confidence, used KnowledgeEvidence ids, unsupported claims, and review findings.

## Quality Gates

- Every feedback item cites a source case, review, failure, report, or
  KnowledgeEvidence item.
- Accepted and rejected examples are labeled separately.
- Failure-derived BugPattern candidates cite execution or report evidence.
- Feedback remains draft until human review approves it.
- Prompt eligibility is never inferred from model confidence alone.

## Forbidden Actions

- Do not call external providers.
- Do not create vector indexes.
- Do not trigger MCP runtime.
- Do not promote generated cases.
- Do not mark feedback as approved or prompt-eligible.
- Do not mutate historical review, failure, report, or TestCase records.

## Tool Permissions

- Read supplied evidence and summaries only.
- No execution tools.
- No provider calls.
- No MCP tools.
